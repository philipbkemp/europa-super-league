Europa Super League

What is the Europa Super League? A long time ago, I wanted to know who was the best team in Europe. Not based on a one-off game, but a league that combined all of Europe. So, with a few choice scripts and some quick maths to work out points per game to hopefully reduce any bias towards the bigger leagues, this is the result! If you really want to dig into my thinking and see a brain dump of the rules and logic I've used, that's on a dedicated rules page.
